About QualiSearch

A Center for Research, Knowledge, and Social Transformation

The QualiSearch Center for Interdisciplinary Research and Development was founded in the Philippines in 2010 by Dr. Von L. Sarino with the vision of creating an institution where research, scholarship, professional development, and social responsibility converge.

From its beginnings as a research-oriented initiative, QualiSearch has developed into a multidisciplinary platform serving researchers, educators, graduate students, academic institutions, professional organizations, government agencies, and communities.

The Center advances research that is methodologically rigorous, ethically grounded, professionally relevant, and responsive to contemporary societal concerns. It promotes interdisciplinary, multidisciplinary, and transdisciplinary collaboration in recognition that complex educational, institutional, technological, cultural, economic, and social challenges cannot be adequately addressed through a single field of knowledge.

QualiSearch supports the complete research and knowledge cycle—from the development of researchers and research projects to scholarly publication, professional collaboration, public communication, and the meaningful utilization of knowledge.

Our Institutional Identity

Who We Are

QualiSearch is an interdisciplinary research and development institution dedicated to connecting people, knowledge, disciplines, institutions, and communities.

The Center provides an integrated environment for:

  • Advancing postgraduate and professional learning
  • Publishing and disseminating scholarly knowledge

Through its constituent units, QualiSearch ensures that research is not only produced, but also evaluated, published, shared, communicated, and applied.

Our History

Founded on a Commitment to Meaningful Research

The QualiSearch Center for Interdisciplinary Research and Development was established in 2010 in the Philippines by Dr. Von L. Sarino.

The organization was founded on the belief that research should extend beyond academic compliance and contribute meaningfully to professional practice, institutional development, public understanding, and social transformation.

Over the years, QualiSearch has expanded its work in research mentoring, graduate research supervision, capacity development, scholarly publishing, professional collaboration, institutional consultancy, and knowledge communication.

Its continuing development reflects a broader institutional commitment: to create credible spaces where researchers can strengthen their competencies, share scholarly work, build professional networks, and communicate knowledge to audiences within and beyond the academic community.

Today, QualiSearch operates through four constituent units that embody its principal areas of institutional innovation.

Vision
To become a globally recognized interdisciplinary research and knowledge institution that advances credible scholarship, professional excellence, innovation, and socially transformative development.
Mission

The QualiSearch Center for Interdisciplinary Research and Development is committed to:

  • Developing the research competencies of scholars, educators, professionals, and institutional leaders
  • Promoting rigorous, ethical, inclusive, and socially responsive research
  • Providing credible platforms for scholarly publication and knowledge dissemination
  • Building interdisciplinary and international research communities
  • Strengthening research leadership, governance, and institutional quality systems
  • Advancing postgraduate professional development and competency-based certification
  • Translating research into accessible, engaging, and socially relevant multimedia formats
  • Supporting the utilization of research in education, policy, professional practice, industry, and community development
  • Promoting responsible innovation and the ethical use of artificial intelligence and emerging technologies
Core Purpose
To advance knowledge and strengthen society by connecting research, professional capability, scholarly communication, and interdisciplinary collaboration.

QualiSearch Institute for Research, Innovation, and Professional Development

QualiSearch Institute for Research, Innovation, and Professional Development

Developing Researchers. Strengthening Institutions. Advancing Professional Practice.

The QualiSearch Institute for Research, Innovation, and Professional Development is the research capacity-building, postgraduate certification, consultancy, and professional learning unit of the Center.

It develops advanced competencies among researchers, educators, graduate students, research advisers, institutional leaders, research directors, quality assurance professionals, and scholarly publishing practitioners.

Its principal areas include:

  • Postgraduate professional certifications
  • Research methodology and data analytics
  • Graduate research supervision and advising
  • Research leadership and administration
  • Research quality assurance
  • Research ethics and integrity
  • Artificial intelligence in research
  • Institutional research consultancy
  • Professional development programs
  • Conferences, seminars, and masterclasses
  • Research innovation and social impact initiatives

The Institute develops the people and systems that make high-quality research possible.

QualiSearch Academic Press

Publishing Credible Scholarship. Expanding Access to Knowledge.

QualiSearch Academic Press is the scholarly publishing and knowledge dissemination unit of the Center.

It provides credible and ethical platforms for publishing, preserving, and increasing the visibility of scholarly work across disciplines.

Its principal areas include:

  • Research manuals and professional publications

  • Editorial and publication services
  • Peer-review management
  • Publication ethics and research integrity
  • DOI and metadata management
  • Digital preservation
  • Journal indexing and discoverability

The Press transforms research outputs into enduring and accessible scholarly contributions.

QualiSearch International Council of Interdisciplinary Researchers

Connecting Researchers. Advancing Knowledge. Transforming Society.

The QualiSearch International Council of Interdisciplinary Researchers is the professional membership, research networking, recognition, and international collaboration unit of the Center.

It brings together researchers, scholars, educators, professionals, institutions, and research leaders who share a commitment to interdisciplinary inquiry and socially responsive scholarship.

  • National, regional, and international chapters
  • Interdisciplinary research clusters
  • Professional and institutional networking
  • Collaborative research initiatives
  • Research awards and recognition
  • Fellowship and professional distinction
  • International conferences and assemblies
  • Researcher development
  • Institutional and global partnerships

The Council builds the professional community through which researchers collaborate, grow, and contribute.

QualiSearch Studios

Transforming Research Into Stories That Inform, Educate, and Inspire.

QualiSearch Studios is the multimedia production, digital storytelling, and public knowledge communication unit of the Center.

  • Researcher and expert interviews
  • Video abstracts and research explainers
  • Educational media and digital learning resources
  • Recorded lectures and masterclasses
  • Audiobooks and narrated publications
  • Multimedia policy briefs
  • Research-based advocacy campaigns
  • Institutional and conference media coverage
  • Public scholarship and digital storytelling

The Studios brings research beyond academic spaces and connects knowledge with wider audiences.

Our Strategic Commitments

What We Stand For

Interdisciplinary Research

QualiSearch promotes collaboration across disciplines, sectors, institutions, and professional communities to address complex and interconnected concerns.

Research Excellence

The Center supports methodological rigor, analytical depth, transparent reporting, and evidence-based scholarly practice.

Ethics and Integrity

QualiSearch advances responsible research conduct, publication ethics, academic integrity, data protection, and accountable use of artificial intelligence.

Professional Development

The Center develops postgraduate-level competencies among researchers, educators, advisers, editors, research managers, and institutional leaders.

Scholarly Communication

QualiSearch provides platforms through which research can be published, preserved, discovered, discussed, and utilized.

Innovation and Digital Transformation

The Center supports the responsible use of emerging technologies and innovative methods in research, education, publishing, and knowledge communication.

Research Utilization and Social Impact

QualiSearch encourages the application of research in policy, education, institutional development, professional practice, industry, and community life.

Inclusion and Sustainability

The Center promotes research that contributes to equitable development, cultural respect, environmental responsibility, and the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.

Why QualiSearch

An Integrated Research and Knowledge Institution

QualiSearch brings together services and opportunities that are often offered separately by training institutions, publishing organizations, professional associations, and media production groups. Institutional Leadership

Dr. Von L. Sarino

Founder, President, and Chief Executive Officer

Dr. Von L. Sarino founded the QualiSearch Center for Interdisciplinary Research and Development in 2010 in the Philippines.

As Founder, President, and Chief Executive Officer, he provides strategic leadership across the Center's research development, professional education, scholarly publishing, international networking, and multimedia knowledge communication initiatives.

He is an educator, graduate professor, research adviser, academic editor, institutional development practitioner, and advocate of interdisciplinary and socially responsive research.

His professional work encompasses:

  • Qualitative and mixed methods research
  • Graduate research supervision
  • Research leadership and governance
  • Institutional research development
  • Scholarly publishing and editorial management
  • Research quality assurance
  • Professional certification and capacity development
  • Responsible use of artificial intelligence in academic and research practice

Under his leadership, QualiSearch continues to build an integrated institution where research is developed, published, connected, communicated, and transformed into meaningful professional and social contributions.
